2057, A Wireless World
Behind the Idea
A Wireless World explores the past uses and methods of communication transmission. The artefacts displayed portray certain historic methods which have been collated with modern day communication techniques to provide an insight into the connection between historic and modern practices. This then poses the idea of how contemporary methods of communication transmission will be perceived as ancient in the future and how the practices we use today will be manipulated and inspire new technologies and transmission methods in the future.
